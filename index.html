About Quantum Crafters

Quantum Crafters is a student-led club dedicated to innovation, tech learning, and hands-on experience in fields like AI, coding, and IoT.

We organize clubs, councils, competitions, and our flagship event, ALGNITE: The CodeQuest. See our event highlight collage below!

Aims & Objectives

  • Foster innovation/creativity through projects, workshops, and competitions
  • Develop practical skills in AI, IoT, programming
  • Build collaborative tech culture with mentorship
  • Industry-ready skills via guest lectures/webinars
  • Host hackathons and tech events
  • Promote research and help students publish papers
